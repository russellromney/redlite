Where Database Operations Become Art

"The Opening"

database_open.png | 10×10 codels | 2024

This piece represents the moment of connection—when code reaches out to storage and a relationship is formed. The warm reds flow into cool blues, symbolizing the transition from request to response. The black barriers represent the boundaries we must respect in our data journey.

Push Execute Syscall

"Persistence"

set_value.png | 10×10 codels | 2024

A meditation on storage. The key (warm tones, top) descends to meet its value (cool tones, bottom). Where they intersect in the central green block, the SET operation occurs. The white space represents the peace that comes from knowing your data is safely stored.

"Retrieval"

get_value.png | 10×10 codels | 2024

Data emerges from darkness. The black border represents the unknown—keys that may or may not exist. The concentric rings of blue draw the eye inward, to the center where the value is found. Or perhaps not found. The art does not tell us; we must run it to discover the truth.

"The Many Becoming One"

hash_operations.png | 10×10 codels | 2024

A celebration of structure. The chaotic rainbow border represents the many fields that can exist within a hash. The calm white center with its organized color blocks represents the structured storage of HSET. Field and value, together as one, within the container of the key.
